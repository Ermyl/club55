Live well. Spend less. Stay independent.

Club 55 is an active retirement community on the Kāpiti Coast – freehold homes at a fair price, shared facilities, and transparent weekly costs in a neighbourhood that feels like home.

Because retirement living in New Zealand has lost its balance — too expensive, too restrictive, and too late in life.

Club 55 was founded to offer something better: freehold homes at a fair price, transparent weekly costs, and a community designed for active people in their 50s and 60s.

And because scale matters, Club 55 is built as a true community — 2,000–3,000 homes, not a small village — which allows better amenities, lower costs, and a neighbourhood that stands on its own.
